About me

Picturesque Ukraine became a cradle for me. Forests, fields, blue lakes - everything permeates my soul with love and light. I see that the world is spread with the color of the rainbow. In my perception - Light fills the space and the Shadows become transparent. I paint watercolors, because watercolors are the air that penetrates everything that surrounds us.

Graduated from the National Academy of Fine Arts and Architecture in Kyiv. Two higher education: painter-restorer of easel painting, architect-painter.

Since 1999 to 2006 he worked at the National Research and Restoration Center of Ukraine, a restorer of icon painting. With trembling in my heart, I restored broken sacred sights by touching the time. Beginning in 2007, I work as an architect.
